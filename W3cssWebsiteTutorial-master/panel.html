The Panel Class

The w3-panel class adds a 16px top and bottom margin and a 16px left and right padding to any HTML element.

Example :

< " >

<div class="w3-panel w3-red">

<p> This will create panel with red color. </p>

</div>

Output :

This will create panel with red color.

Panels for Notes

The w3-panel class is perfect for displaying notes.

Notes are often displayed with a pale color.

London is most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a metropolitian area of over 9 million inhabitants .

Example :

<div class="w3-panel w3-pale-green">

<p>London is most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a metropolitian area of over 9 million inhabitants .</p>

</div>

Output :

London is most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a metropolitian area of over 9 million inhabitants .

Panels for Quotes

The w3-container class is perfect for displaying Quotes.

"Slow but Steady wins the Race."

Example :

<div class="w3-panel w3-leftbar w3-sand w3-xxlarge w3-serif">

<p><i>"Slow but Steady wins the Race."</i></p>

</div>

Output :

"Slow but Steady wins the Race."

Panels for Alerts

The w3-panel class is perfect for displaying Alerts.

Alerts are often displayed with strong colors.

Danger

Red often indicates a dangerous or negative situation.

Example :

<div class="w3-panel w3-red">

<h3>Danger</h3>

<p>Red often indicates a dangerous or negative situation.</h3>

</div>

Output :

Danger

Red often indicates a dangerous or negative situation
